## DESIGN BRIEF

**Visual identity:**
- Primary color: Deep Ocean Teal `#0B6E4F`
- Accent: Warm Sand `#E8C97A`
- Alert/Action: Coral Red `#D84040`
- Background: Off-white `#F8F6F0`
- Text: Charcoal `#1E1E1E`
- Font (heading): Playfair Display or Lora (serif, editorial weight)
- Font (body): Inter or DM Sans (clean, readable)

**Tone:** Urgent but hopeful. Poetic about Kuakata's beauty. Factual about the crisis. Bold and national in scale.

**Feel:** This is not a small local cleanup. This is Bangladesh's flagship environmental moment of 2026.

**Hero imagery suggestions:**
- Full-width aerial of Kuakata shoreline (sunrise side)
- Side-by-side split: pristine beach vs. plastic-covered shoreline
- Volunteer silhouettes at golden hour

### SECTION 4 — ABOUT THE EVENT

**Section label (small caps, teal):**
```
WHAT IS THIS
```

**Headline:**
```
Bangladesh's Biggest Beach Clean-Up.
Not just an event — a movement.
```

**Body copy:**
```
The Plastic-Free Kuakata Initiative is a national-scale, community-powered
environmental action organized by Active Mind and QualFix on World
Environment Day, June 5, 2026.

Kuakata — locally known as "Shagor Konna" (Daughter of the Sea) — is one
of the rarest beaches on Earth: the only place in Bangladesh where visitors
witness both sunrise and sunset from the same shore. It sits beside the
Gangamati and Fatra Reserved Mangrove Forests, home to rare dolphins,
migratory birds, and the endangered red crab.

But today, plastic bottles, ghost fishing nets, polythene bags, and
construction debris choke its shoreline. Tourist numbers are falling.
Fishermen's livelihoods are shrinking. The ecosystem is under threat.

This is our answer.
```

**Three icon cards (horizontal row):**

1. **🧹 The Clean-Up**

   School outreach, media coverage, social media campaigns, and community workshops to build lasting behavior change.

3. **🤝 The Partnership**
   Bringing together youth, corporations, NGOs, government bodies, and international organizations under one mission.

## TECHNICAL NOTES FOR DEVELOPER

- **Single HTML page** with smooth anchor scroll between sections
- Countdown timer: JavaScript, targeting `2026-06-05T07:00:00+06:00`
- Volunteer form: Google Form embed or Tally.so (free, clean design)
- Sponsor form: Separate Google Form with extra fields
- Cookie-free, fast-loading — target audience includes mobile users on 4G
- OpenGraph meta tags for Facebook sharing: event image, title, description
- Favicon: wave emoji or custom teal wave SVG
- Analytics: Google Analytics 4 + Meta Pixel for Facebook ad tracking
- Languages: English primary, consider Bengali toggle
- Mobile-first design — most Bangladesh traffic is mobile
- WhatsApp floating button (bottom right): quick volunteer query shortcut
