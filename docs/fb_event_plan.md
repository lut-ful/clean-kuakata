## CONTENT PILLARS (5 types of posts, rotate weekly)

### Pillar 1 — 🚨 THE CRISIS (Educate)
Facts, data, news about Kuakata's environmental situation.
Goal: Make people feel the urgency. Make them care before they act.

### Pillar 2 — 🌅 THE BEAUTY (Inspire)
Stunning photos and videos of Kuakata — sunrises, sunsets, dolphins, red crabs.
Goal: Remind people what we're protecting. Emotional connection.

### Pillar 3 — 🙋 VOLUNTEER STORIES (Community)
Profiles of volunteers, youth leaders, schools joining.
Goal: Social proof. Make people want to be part of the group.

### Pillar 4 — 🤝 PARTNER SPOTLIGHTS (Credibility)
Sponsor and NGO announcements. UNDP, BD Clean, Bangladesh Tourism Board.
Goal: Build trust. Signal national scale. Attract more sponsors.

### Pillar 5 — 📣 CALLS TO ACTION (Convert)
Direct asks: register, share, donate, spread the word.
Goal: Turn followers into participants.
