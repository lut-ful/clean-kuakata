## CONTENT STRATEGY

### The Instagram Grid — Visual Identity

Instagram is a visual portfolio. Plan the grid in 3-column rows.
Each row tells a story. Use this pattern:

```
ROW 1 (Launch): [Kuakata Beauty] [Crisis Fact] [Event Graphic]
ROW 2 (Week 2): [Wildlife]      [Volunteer]   [Sponsor/Partner]
ROW 3 (Week 3): [Behind Scenes] [Quote card]  [Countdown]
ROW 4 (Week 4): [Beach photo]   [Team photo]  [Call to Action]
ROW 5 (Event):  [Live photo 1]  [Live photo 2] [Impact graphic]
```

**Color consistency per content type:**
- Crisis/data posts → Dark teal `#0B4F37` background, coral accent
- Beauty/nature posts → Light, airy, real photography
- Event info posts → Sand `#E8C97A` background, teal text
- Volunteer posts → Coral `#D84040` accent, warm tone
- Sponsor posts → Clean white card on teal background

## INSTAGRAM-SPECIFIC STRATEGY NOTES

### Reels best practices:
- Hook in first 1–2 seconds (text overlay or spoken words)
- Add captions/subtitles — 85% of Instagram video is watched without sound
- Use trending audio from Instagram's library when possible
- Post Reels at 6–9 PM BST for highest Bangladesh engagement
- Always end with a clear single CTA (one action only)

### Story best practices:
- Post 3–7 stories per day in the final 2 weeks
- Use countdown stickers (Instagram has a built-in countdown feature — use it for June 5)
- Poll stickers, question boxes, and quiz stickers boost engagement
- Add location tag: Kuakata Beach on every event-related story
- Add event hashtag sticker on every story

### Influencer strategy:
Target 10–15 Bangladeshi influencers in these categories:
- Environmental / travel content creators (even small accounts, 10K–100K)
- University/student lifestyle influencers
- Food & travel bloggers who cover Bangladesh destinations
- Photography accounts that feature Bangladesh nature

Approach: Personal DM with a genuine message + offer of free event access + media accreditation + T-shirt.

### Hashtag sets by post type:

**Nature/beauty posts:**
`#Kuakata #ShagorKonna #DaughterOfTheSea #Bangladesh #CoastalBangladesh
#BangladeshBeach #BayOfBengal #Sunrise #Sunset #NaturePhotography`

**Volunteer/action posts:**
`#PlasticFreeKuakata #BeachCleanup #Volunteer #VolunteerBangladesh
#NowForClimate #ClimateAction #WorldEnvironmentDay2026 #SaveKuakata`

**Wildlife posts:**
`#MarineLife #MarineConservation #Bangladesh #RedCrab #OceanLife
#Biodiversity #WildlifeBangladesh #SaveOurOcean`

**Sponsor/corporate posts:**
`#CSR #CorporateSocialResponsibility #GreenBusiness #Sustainability
#PlasticFreeKuakata #Bangladesh #ClimateLeadership`

**Use 10–15 hashtags per post (Instagram optimum). Mix popular + niche.**
