# CleanKuakata: A Long-Term Mission

Plastic-Free Kuakata 2026 is not just a beach clean-up. It is the beginning of something that will outlast June 5.

**CleanKuakata** is the initiative behind the event — a long-term commitment, co-founded by **Active Mind** and **QualFix**, to restore and protect Kuakata's coastline through:

- **Annual clean-up events** on World Environment Day each June
- **Year-round monitoring** of beach plastic levels and ecosystem health
- **Policy advocacy** for permanent waste infrastructure in the Kalapara Upazila
- **Community education** with local schools, fishermen, and hospitality businesses
- **Open data publishing** — every kilogram we collect becomes part of a public dataset

## Why Long-Term

A single event changes a beach for a day. A sustained initiative changes behaviour, policy, and infrastructure.

Bangladesh has the resources, the youth, and the motivation to become a model for coastal conservation in South Asia. Kuakata — with its unique geography, its mangroves, its rare wildlife, and its tourism potential — is the right place to prove it.

CleanKuakata 2027 is already being planned. And 2028. And beyond.

## How You Fit In

Whether you volunteer on June 5, sponsor the event, or simply share this page — you are part of this initiative. Your name, your organisation, your brand is associated with Bangladesh's most serious environmental commitment of this decade.

The sea doesn't stop needing us when the event ends.

**Neither do we.**
